The Zimele Racism Reporting App(ZiRRA) is developed by Ahmed Kathrada Foundation.

This App works as a racism incident reporting tool that allows Individuals to report cases of racism countrywide. The App has following functions in that:

  • It is a user-friendly way of reporting racism in South Africa
  • It enables those who experience or witness racism to do something about it and speak out about this injustice.

Essentially, this App is designed to encourage citizens to speak out and report acts of racism so that they can be investigated and proper measures taken to address these injustices.
